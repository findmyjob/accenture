Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a Naukri profile? ▼

Creating a Naukri profile is simple and free:

  • Click on "Register" on the homepage
  • Enter your email ID and create a password
  • Fill in your personal details
  • Add your work experience and education
  • Upload your resume
  • Complete your profile with skills and preferences

A complete profile gets 3x more views from recruiters!

What are key skills and how do I add them? ▼

Key skills are the specific abilities that make you suitable for a job. They include:

  • Technical skills (programming languages, software)
  • Soft skills (communication, leadership)
  • Industry-specific knowledge
  • Certifications and tools you're familiar with

Add skills by going to your profile and clicking "Add Skills". Choose from suggestions or add custom skills.

How can I make my profile more attractive to recruiters? ▼

Follow these tips to improve your profile visibility:

  • Add a professional profile photo
  • Write a compelling profile summary
  • Include relevant keywords in your profile
  • Add detailed work experience with achievements
  • Keep your profile updated regularly
  • Upload an updated resume
How do I search for jobs on Naukri? ▼

Searching for jobs is easy:

  • Use the search bar on the homepage
  • Enter job title, skills, or company name
  • Select your preferred location
  • Choose your experience level
  • Apply filters for salary, company type, etc.
  • Browse through relevant job listings
What are job alerts and how do I set them up? ▼

Job alerts notify you when new jobs matching your criteria are posted:

  • Go to "Job Alerts" in your account
  • Click "Create Job Alert"
  • Set your preferences (location, salary, skills)
  • Choose alert frequency (daily/weekly)
  • Save your alert

You can create multiple alerts for different job types.

How do I use advanced search filters? ▼

Advanced filters help you find more relevant jobs:

  • Company size and type
  • Industry and functional area
  • Salary range
  • Job posting date
  • Work from home options
  • Part-time/full-time preferences
How do I apply for jobs on Naukri? ▼

Applying for jobs is straightforward:

  • Click on a job that interests you
  • Read the job description carefully
  • Click "Apply Now" button
  • Choose the resume you want to send
  • Add a cover letter if required
  • Submit your application
How can I track my job applications? ▼

Track all your applications in one place:

  • Go to "My Jobs" section
  • Click on "Applied Jobs"
  • View application status: Applied, Viewed, Shortlisted
  • Filter by date or company
  • Get notifications on status updates
What does each application status mean? ▼

Understanding application statuses:

  • Applied: Your application has been submitted
  • Viewed: Recruiter has viewed your profile
  • Shortlisted: You've been selected for next round
  • Not Shortlisted: Application was not selected
What is Naukri Premium and how does it help? ▼

Naukri Premium offers enhanced features:

  • Priority display of your profile
  • Access to premium job postings
  • Direct recruiter contact details
  • Enhanced profile visibility
  • Application tracking and insights
How do recommended jobs work? ▼

Recommended jobs are personalized suggestions based on:

  • Your profile information
  • Previous job searches
  • Skills and experience
  • Job preferences
  • Application history

Keep your profile updated for better recommendations.

How do I manage my privacy settings? ▼

Control your privacy with these settings:

  • Go to "Settings" in your account
  • Choose "Privacy Settings"
  • Control profile visibility to recruiters
  • Manage email notifications
  • Set communication preferences
How do I hide my profile from my current employer? ▼

To maintain confidentiality:

  • Go to "Settings" > "Privacy"
  • Select "Block companies"
  • Add your current employer's name
  • Your profile won't be visible to them
  • You can add multiple companies to block
How do I keep my account secure? ▼

Follow these security best practices:

  • Use a strong, unique password
  • Don't share your login credentials
  • Log out from public computers
  • Be cautious of phishing emails
  • Update your password regularly
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
How do I identify fake job postings? ▼

Watch out for these red flags:

  • Jobs asking for upfront payment
  • Unrealistic salary offers
  • Poor grammar in job descriptions
  • Requests for personal financial information
  • Interviews via chat or SMS only
  • Jobs requiring you to purchase products

Always verify company details and report suspicious postings.
